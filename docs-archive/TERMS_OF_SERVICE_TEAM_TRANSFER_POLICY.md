## Team Membership and Content Transfer

### Automatic Transfer Upon Team Joining

When you accept an invitation to join a team or upgrade your subscription to a Team or Custom plan, the following automatic transfers occur:

#### 1. Story Ownership Transfer

**All of your personal stories will automatically become team stories.** This includes:

- ‚úÖ **Included**: All stories you created in your personal account
- ‚ùå **Excluded**: Community story remixes (these remain your personal copies)

**Important**: This transfer is **permanent and irreversible**. Once transferred:
- Stories become shared with all team members
- Team members can view, edit, and manage these stories
- You cannot transfer stories back to your personal account
- If you leave the team, stories remain with the team

#### 2. Credit Transfer

**All of your personal credits will be transferred to the team credit pool.** This includes:

- All unused credits from purchases
- All credits from your personal subscription
- Trial credits (if applicable)

**Important**: Credits are **permanently transferred** to the team:
- You cannot retrieve these credits if you leave the team
- Credits become shared with all team members
- Team admin controls credit allocation

#### 3. Subscription Cancellation

**Your personal subscription will be automatically canceled** if you have:

- An active Individual plan subscription, OR
- An active Trial subscription

**What happens**:
- Your individual subscription ends at the current period end
- No refunds are provided for unused subscription time
- You will use the team's subscription instead
- Your access continues uninterrupted through the team

### Team Subscription Upgrades

When you upgrade from an Individual or Trial plan to a Team or Custom plan:

#### Automatic Team Creation

If you don't already have a team:
- A team will be automatically created for you
- You will be the team owner
- The team name will be "{Your Name}'s Team"

#### Automatic Transfer

### Leaving a Team

If you voluntarily leave a team or are removed:

#### What You Lose

- **Stories**: All stories transferred to the team remain with the team
- **Credits**: All credits transferred to the team remain in the team pool
- **Subscription**: You lose access to the team's subscription

#### What Happens Next

- You revert to a personal account with no subscription
- You must subscribe individually or join another team to create new stories
- Previous team stories remain accessible to the team, not to you

### Team Deletion

If a team is deleted by the owner:

- **Story Ownership**: Currently undefined behavior (implementation pending)
- **Credit Refunds**: No automatic refunds to former team members
- **Recommendation**: Transfer stories back to owner before deletion (feature pending)

### Your Rights and Responsibilities

#### Before Accepting a Team Invitation

You have the right to:
- Review these terms before accepting
- See how many stories and credits will be transferred
- Decline the invitation if you don't agree

#### As a Team Member

You are responsible for:
- Understanding that transfers are permanent
- Coordinating with your team on content management
- Backing up important stories before leaving a team

#### As a Team Owner

You are responsible for:
- Managing team member access appropriately
- Communicating transfer policies to invitees
- Ensuring proper use of team credits

### Data Protection and Privacy

#### Story Content

- Team stories are accessible to all team members
- Team members can view all story content, images, and audio
- You grant team members permission to modify team stories
- Content remains subject to our Privacy Policy

#### Credit Usage

- Team admins can view credit transaction history
- Individual credit usage may be tracked for billing purposes
- Team admins cannot transfer credits back to individuals

### Modifications to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be effective:

- Immediately for new team joins or subscription upgrades
- After 30 days notice for existing team members

Continued use of the service after policy changes constitutes acceptance.
